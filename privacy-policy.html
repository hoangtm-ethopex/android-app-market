This page explains how this website collects, uses, and protects information when you visit.

  • Analytics: aggregated metrics such as page views, time on page, traffic sources, and popular pages to improve content and user experience.
  • Cookies / local storage: may be used to remember certain display preferences or support analytics.

How we use information

  • Improve layout, performance, and reading experience.

  • Detect and prevent spam, abuse, or technical issues.

Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. Analytics and operational data may be processed by third-party services (for example, analytics providers or hosting) under their own policies, solely to operate and measure the site.

Data retention

Technical logs and analytics are retained for a reasonable period for operations and analysis. Exact retention may vary depending on server/service configuration.

Your choices and rights

  • Request support: you can contact us to ask about data practices or request updates/deletion where applicable.
