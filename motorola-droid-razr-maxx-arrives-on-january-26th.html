Motorola Droid Razr MAXX arrives on January 26th

The much awaited phone Motorola Droid Maxx is finally gonna be released on January 26th.  Initially the device will be available for purchase only through Verizon Wireless Network's stores. The Razr Maxx is a larger version of the original Motorola Droid Razr. The main improvements are in the dimensions and battery. Motorola claims that it is the long lasting phone which is impossibly thin. The 3300 mAh battery is expected to power up the device for a straight 21 hours of talk time. Even if the device is released with Gingerbread OS, the official Ice cream Sandwich update will be released soon. With 8.99mm thickness, it is one of the thinnest smartphones ever released.

 

Features:

OS: Android 2.3.5 (Gingerbread) Android 4.0 (Ice Cream Sandwich) upgradeable

CAMERA: 8 MP (rear facing camera)

VIDEO CAPTURE: 1080p HD, tri-microphone for audio scene selection, 1920X1080 resolution, up to 30fps capture and playback

TALK TIME: Up to 21.5 hours of continuous talk time.

BATTERY TYPE: 3300 mAh Li Ion

STANDBY TIME: Up to 380 hrs.

DISPLAY SIZE: 4.3-in.; Super AMOLED Advanced qHD(540 x 960)

CPU: Dual-core 1.2 GHz Cortex-A9
