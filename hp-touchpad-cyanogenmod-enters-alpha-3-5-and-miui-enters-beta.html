HP Touchpad- CyanogenMod enters alpha 3.5 and MIUI enters beta

Good news for HP Touchpad owners, the modders are still working to port android ROMS to the HP’s sensational WebOS tablet, HP Touchpad. The popular android custom ROM CyanogenMod enters alpha 3.5. This version comes up with a lot of improvements in the User Interface and adds the ability to render 3D images. Thus the overall improvements will be in the appearance of UI and gaming experience.

The HP touchpad build of very popular android version MIUI enters its beta level. The only known bug with this version is the absence of an on screen menu. Developers are still working on it and we can expect that it will get rectified in the next release.

You can download Cyanogen and MIUI versions for Touchpad from the RootzWiki
